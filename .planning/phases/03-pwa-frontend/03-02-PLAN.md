<objective>
Build the pure, clock-injected derivation and de-DE formatting layer — the testable heart of the
PWA where every locked correctness decision lives (best-deal selection, upcoming/active split,
per-store staleness, card sort, and all de-DE number/date formatting). No DOM, no fetch: pure
functions taking (data, now/today) and returning plain values, unit-tested with node --test in the
repo's existing style.

layer (Plan 04) and chart (Plan 03) can trust the derived facts. This is the layer most likely to
silently misbehave (a dead store looking fresh, a needsReview offer winning the hero, a hardcoded
weekday) — so it is isolated and TDD-driven.
Output: web/src/derive/derive.js + web/src/format/format.js with passing web/test/*.test.mjs suites.
</objective>
